Mendeley Reference Manager v2.57.0

You can now:

  • Copy a reference as a BibTeX entry by right clicking on a reference whilst in your personal library or group and selecting the Copy BibTeX Entry option

We fixed an issue where:

  • If you are on a slow connection, the sync would interrupt keystrokes when adding/editing a sticky note or adding/editing a Notebook page
  • Screen readers were incorrectly reading out the smart collections

Support

  • If you encounter any problems using Mendeley or have questions to ask please contact our support team.
  • For news and updates about Mendeley see our blog.

Released: 15 September 2021
